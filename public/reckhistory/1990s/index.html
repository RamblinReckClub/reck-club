Important Events

  • 1990 Final Four

    March 1990 (Denver, CO)

    The Reck follows the Yellow Jacket basketball team to Denver for the Final Four, where the team plays a national semifinal versus UNLV. The Reck was freighted by van from Atlanta to Denver, a 1400-mile journey and one of the farthest trips from home for the Reck.

    Learn More »

  • 1991 Citrus Bowl

    January 1, 1991 (Miami, FL)

    The Reck travels to the Citrus Bowl and watches as Tech clinches a share of the 1990 National Championship, the Institute's 4th football title.

  • 1990 Olympic Bid parade

    September 1990 (Atlanta, GA)

    After Atlanta wins the right to host the 1996 Olympics Games, the Reck participates in the victory parade along with students from the Georgia Tech Olympic Coalition.

    Learn More »

  • National Championship parade

    1991 (Atlanta, GA)

    The Reck helps celebrate the 1990 Yellow Jacket football team and its national championship during a parade and subsequent event at Bobby Dodd Stadium.

  • Hapeville plant ends relationship with Reck Club

    1994 (Atlanta, GA)

    The Hapeville Ford plant terminates its agreement with Reck Club. All responsibility for repairs and maintenance for the Reck now falls to Reck Club members, along with some help from local garages.

  • Major repair

    1994 (Atlanta, GA)

    In the first major project after the Hapeville Ford plant's termination of services, Reck Club helps restore the Reck's wheels and brakes to original Model A specifications.
